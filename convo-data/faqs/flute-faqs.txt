Q: What is a flute made of?
A: Most are made of silver, nickel, or gold; beginner models can be plastic.

Q: How does a flute produce sound?
A: By splitting the airstream over the edge of the mouth hole.

Q: How many holes does a concert flute have?
A: Typically 16 keys with open or closed holes depending on the model.

Q: Can children learn the flute?
A: Yes, starting as early as age 7 with a properly sized flute.
