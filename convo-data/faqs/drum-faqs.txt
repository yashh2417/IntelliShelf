Q: What is a drum made of?
A: Drums typically consist of a wooden or metal shell and synthetic or animal skin drumheads.

Q: How do you tune a drum?
A: By tightening or loosening the tension rods around the drumhead to change pitch.

Q: Can drums be used in classical music?
A: Yes, drums like timpani and snare drums are essential in orchestras.

Q: What is a common size for a snare drum?
A: Around 14 inches in diameter and 5.5 inches deep.
