Q: What types of guitars are there?
A: Acoustic, classical, electric, and bass guitars.

Q: How do you change guitar strings?
A: Remove old strings, thread new ones through the bridge and tuners, then tighten and tune.

Q: What is standard guitar tuning?
A: E-A-D-G-B-E from lowest to highest string.

Q: What is a capo used for?
A: It clamps on the fretboard to change the key without altering finger positions.
